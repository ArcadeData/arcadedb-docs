==== Settings Panel

The Settings Panel allows you to customize the appearance of ArcadeDB Studio.

image::studio-settings.png[Settings Panel]

[discrete]
===== Theme

Choose between three theme options:

* *Light* - A light color scheme (default)
* *Dark* - A dark color scheme for reduced eye strain
* *System* - Automatically matches your operating system's theme preference

The selected theme is saved in your browser and persists across sessions.
